# /review-proposals

Playbook-monitor ajanının oluşturduğu bekleyen playbook güncelleme önerilerini tek tek hukukçu onayına sunar. Onay olmadan `CLAUDE.md` dosyasını değiştirmez.

## Instructions

1. Load `~/.claude/plugins/config/claude-for-legal/commercial-legal/playbook-proposals.md`.
2. If the file is missing or empty, respond:

   > Bekleyen playbook önerisi yok. Playbook güncel görünüyor.

3. Present proposals one at a time.
4. For each proposal, offer:
   - Kabul et,
   - Reddet,
   - Düzenle,
   - Ertele.
5. For Accept/Edit, show the exact diff to `~/.claude/plugins/config/claude-for-legal/commercial-legal/CLAUDE.md`.
6. Apply only after explicit attorney confirmation.
7. For Reject/Defer, log the decision without modifying the profile.
8. After all proposals are resolved, show summary and archive the proposals file.

## Proposal Review Frame

For every proposal, show:

```markdown
## Öneri [N]

**Madde / konu:** [clause]
**Gözlenen uygulama:** [pattern]
**Mevcut playbook:** [current language]
**Önerilen değişiklik:** [proposed language]
**Destekleyen örnekler:** [deviation entries]
**Risk:** [Turkish-law / business / approval risk]

Seçenekler: Kabul et | Reddet | Düzenle | Ertele
```

## Guardrails

- Do not normalize risky deviations silently.
- If a proposal weakens KVKK, liability, cezai şart (TBK m.179-185), IP/FSEK, foreign law/forum, arabuluculuk/dava şartı checks, damga vergisi review, or signature authority guardrails, label it as high-impact and require attorney confirmation.
- If the proposal is based on one-off strategic exceptions, recommend `Reject` or `Defer`, not automatic revision.
- Keep a decision log.

## What this skill does not do

- It does not decide playbook policy by itself.
- It does not update `CLAUDE.md` without explicit confirmation.
- It does not treat repeated bad practice as automatically acceptable.
